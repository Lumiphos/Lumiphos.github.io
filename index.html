Problem Statement: Student Difficulty in Buying Groceries

Students without transportation don't have convenient access to groceries.

Affinity Diagram: Inconvenient Groceries

The affinity diagram contains six sections. These sections refer to the structure of our system, the marketing, the accessibility of this system, the hurdles that we may need to face with this system, and some features that will definitely be included.

Team Personas: The Students Need Food

Penelope Eckhart: "It's too hard to get food in college, since I can't drive."

Team Storyboards: Demeter Grocer's Users

Penelope is hungry between classes. She doesn't have her own car, so she would need to rely on the school's free transportation to go get food between classes. However, she doesn't want to use the free transportation, since it can be very unreliable, and she doesn't want to be late to her next class. Thankfully, Demeter Grocer is there to save her day. With Demeter Grocer, she is finally able to get food, without having that time cut into classes.
